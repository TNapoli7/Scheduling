## How do I create my first organization?

During the registration process, you'll be invited to create an organization. Choose a name that represents your pharmacy, clinic, or laboratory. You can always change the name later in settings.

## How do I invite employees?

1. Go to **Employees**
2. Click **Add Employee**
3. Enter the employee's email and name
4. Choose the position and contract type
5. Click **Invite**

The employee will receive an email with a link to register on the platform.

## What's the difference between Fulltime and Part-time?

- **Fulltime**: Employees with full-time hours (typically 40h/week)
- **Part-time**: Employees with reduced hours (less than 40h/week)

The platform uses this information to generate fair and balanced schedules.

## Can I set my organization's operating hours?

Yes! In **Settings > Operating Hours**, you can define:
- Operating days (Mon-Sun)
- Opening time
- Closing time
- Break intervals

## How does automatic schedule generation work?

Shiftera uses a fairness algorithm that considers:
- Employee availability
- Personal preferences
- Work hour balance
- Rest periods

Just import your employees, set availability, and click **Generate Schedule**.

## Is there a limit to how many employees I can add?

No limit! You can add as many employees as needed. Cost varies only with the number of active platform users.

## How does the trial period work?

You get full access to all features for **14 days** without needing a credit card. After that, choose a paid plan to continue.

## Can I test the platform with dummy data?

Yes! We recommend creating some test employees to understand how the platform works before adding real data.
