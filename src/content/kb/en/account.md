## How do I change my password?

1. Click your profile photo (top right corner)
2. Select **Account Settings**
3. Click **Change Password**
4. Enter your current and new password
5. Click **Save**

## I forgot my password. What do I do?

1. Go to the login page
2. Click **Forgot Password**
3. Enter your email
4. Check your email for a reset link
5. Follow the link and set a new password

## How do I update my profile?

1. Click your profile photo
2. Select **My Profile**
3. Edit your name, email, or photo
4. Click **Save Changes**

## Can I change my organization's name?

Yes! If you're an admin:
1. Go to **Settings > Organization Info**
2. Click **Edit Name**
3. Enter the new name
4. Click **Save**

## How do I set notification preferences?

1. Go to **Settings > Notifications**
2. Choose which notifications you want to receive:
   - Schedule alerts
   - Swap requests
   - Colleague messages
   - System updates
3. Click **Save**

## What's your privacy policy?

Your data is protected under GDPR. We don't share information with third parties without consent. For more details, see our complete **Privacy Policy**.

## How do I export my data?

To request a copy of your personal data:
1. Go to **Settings > Privacy & Data**
2. Click **Request Copy of My Data**
3. You'll receive an email with your data within 5 business days

## Can I delete my account?

Yes, but it's permanent. To delete your account:
1. Go to **Settings > Delete Account**
2. Confirm you understand this is irreversible
3. Click **Delete Permanently**

All data will be removed after 30 days.

## How do I change the platform language?

1. Go to **Settings > Preferences**
2. Select your preferred language:
   - Portuguese
   - English
   - Spanish
3. The platform will reload in the new language
