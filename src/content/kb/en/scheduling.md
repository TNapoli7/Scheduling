## How do I generate a new schedule?

1. Go to **Schedules**
2. Click **Generate New Schedule**
3. Select the period (week or month)
4. Review the generated schedule
5. Click **Publish**

## How do I edit an already published schedule?

1. Go to **Schedules**
2. Find the schedule you want to edit
3. Click **Edit**
4. Make the necessary changes
5. Click **Save**

All employees will be notified of any changes.

## How do I set employee availability?

1. Go to **Availability**
2. For each employee, select the days and hours they're available
3. Mark as unavailable any day they cannot work
4. Click **Save**

Availability is used to generate accurate schedules.

## How are work hours distributed?

Shiftera's fairness algorithm ensures:
- Balanced hour distribution
- Respect for contracts (fulltime vs part-time)
- Balance between morning and evening shifts
- Minimum consecutive days off

## What is a shift?

A shift is a continuous work period, for example:
- Morning shift: 8am - 2pm
- Afternoon shift: 2pm - 8pm
- Night shift: 8pm - 8am (next day)

You can create custom shifts in **Settings > Shifts**.

## Can I block certain days so they won't be scheduled?

Yes! Use the **Time Off** or **Break** features:
- **Scheduled Days Off**: For known vacation periods
- **Break Time**: For regular rest between shifts

## How does shift swapping work?

1. Employee A proposes a swap in **Swaps**
2. Employee B accepts or rejects
3. Manager approves or rejects
4. If approved, the schedule is automatically updated

## Can I export the schedule?

Yes! In **Schedules**, click **Export** to download:
- PDF (for printing)
- Excel (for additional edits)
- CSV (for integration with other systems)

## What do I do if there's a conflict in the schedule?

The platform alerts about conflicts (e.g., employee scheduled without availability). You can:
1. Manually reassign the shift
2. Regenerate the entire schedule
3. Adjust availability and try again
