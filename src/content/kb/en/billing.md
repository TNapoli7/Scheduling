## What's the pricing structure?

Shiftera uses a simple pricing model:
- **Base**: 19€/month (includes full organization)
- **Per active user**: 2€/user/month

Example: For 5 users = 19€ + (5 × 2€) = 29€/month

## Is there a trial period?

Yes! You get **14 days of full free access** without a credit card. After that, choose a paid plan.

## How do I change plans?

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > My Plan**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select the new plan
4. The change takes effect immediately

You'll be adjusted proportionally if you change during a billing period.

## How do I cancel my subscription?

1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Provide a reason (optional)
4. Click **Confirm Cancellation**

Your organization will remain accessible until the end of your current billing period.

## What's the refund policy?

We offer a **full refund within 30 days** if you're not satisfied. Just contact our support.

## How do I update my billing information?

1. Go to **Settings > Billing > Payment Method**
2. Click **Edit Card**
3. Update your credit card
4. Click **Save**

## How much does it cost to add an employee mid-month?

The cost is calculated proportionally. If you add an employee mid-month, you'll only pay for the remaining days.

## What payment methods do you accept?

Currently we accept:
- Credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard)
- PayPal
- Bank transfer (for large orders)

Soon we'll add MB Way and other local options.

## How do I receive my invoice?

All invoices are emailed on the first day of each month. You can also access your invoices in:
**Settings > Billing > Invoice History**

## What if I move to another country?

Currency conversion is done automatically. If you move outside the EU, there may be different tax laws. Contact us for more information.

## How do promotional credits work?

If you received a promotional code:
1. Go to **Settings > Billing**
2. Click **Use Promo Code**
3. Enter the code
4. The credit will be applied immediately

Credits expire after 12 months.
